About Benji



Benji is an imaginative 19 year old artist from Little Village, Chicago. He's inspired by Gherbo's realness, Kid Cudi's cosmic themes, and Lil Peep's defiance. After dropping out of high school to pursue his dreams, Benji has played with Chicago's hip hop boundaries with songs like "Menace", "Monster", and "WarZone". Chicago has listened and it wants more.
This is only the beginning for Benji.
